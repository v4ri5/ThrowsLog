# Athlete-Training-Log

Athlete Training Log is a project designed to help athletes and coaches capture, review, and manage training progress over time. It provides a structured way to record workouts, monitor performance, and analyze training data for better planning and consistency.

## Purpose

- Record training sessions with exercises, sets, reps, load, and notes.
- Track progress across workouts to identify trends and improvements.
- Maintain a historical log of exercises and session details for planning future training.

## Key Features

- Session logging: store date, workout type, and individual exercise details.
- Exercise details: log sets, repetitions, weight, rest, and custom notes.
- Progress tracking: compare past workouts and review improvements.
- Simple organization: keep training data in one place for easy review.

## Typical Workflow

1. Create a new training session entry.
2. Add exercises performed during the session.
3. Record sets, reps, load, and any observations or notes.
4. Review past sessions to identify progress or adjust training plans.

## Benefits

- Encourages consistency by making it easy to log every workout.
- Provides a reference for how an athlete is progressing over time.
- Helps with planning future workouts based on past performance.
- Supports athletes who want a lightweight, easy-to-use training journal.
